# British Library Board (Power to Borrow) Act 2021, 
# 2021 CHAPTER 15, 
An Act to provide the British Library Board with a power to borrow money., 
[29th April 2021], 
Be it enacted by the Queen’s most Excellent Majesty, by and with the adviceand consent of the Lords Spiritual and Temporal, and Commons, in this presentParliament assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:—, 
## 1Power of the British Library Board to borrow money, 
In paragraph 11(1) of the Schedule to the British Library Act 1972 (incidentalpowers), omit “; but the Board shall not have power to borrow money”., 
## 2Extent, commencement and short title, 
(1)This Act extends to England and Wales, Scotland and Northern Ireland., 
(2)This Act comes into force at the end of the period of two months beginningwith the day on which it is passed., 
(3)This Act may be cited as the British Library Board (Power to Borrow) Act2021.,
