# Public Trustee (Liability and Fees) Act 2002, 
# 2002 CHAPTER 35, 
An Act to amend the Public Trustee Act 1906 in respect of the liability andfees of the Public Trustee; and for connected purposes., 
[7th November 2002], 
Be it enacted by the Queen’s most Excellent Majesty, by and with the adviceand consent of the Lords Spiritual and Temporal, and Commons, in this presentParliament assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:—, 
## 1Liability, 
(1)Section 7 of the Public Trustee Act [1906 (c.55)](/id/ukpga/1906/55?view=plain "Go to item of legislation") (liability ofConsolidated Fund) is repealed., 
(2)In section 40(2)(h) of the Crown Proceedings Act [1947 (c.44)](/id/ukpga/1947/44?view=plain "Go to item of legislation") (saving forliability imposed by the 1906 Act), the words “or on the Consolidated Fund ofthe United Kingdom” are repealed., 
(3)Paragraph (a) of section 3(6) of the Public Trustee and Administration ofFunds Act [1986 (c. 57)](/id/ukpga/1986/57?view=plain "Go to item oflegislation") (section 7 of the 1906 Act not to apply) is repealed., 
## 2Fees, 
(1)In section 9 of the Public Trustee Act 1906 (fees charged by publictrustee)—, 
(a)in subsection (1), for the words from “as the Treasury with” to the endthere is substituted “as the Lord Chancellor may fix.”,, 
(b)in subsection (3) the words “, under the regulations of the Treasury,” arerepealed, and, 
(c)subsection (4) is repealed., 
(2)In section 138 of the Law of Property Act [1925 (c.20)](/id/ukpga/1925/20?view=plain "Go to item of legislation") (power tonominate a trust corporation to receive notices), in subsection (11) for “bythe Treasury, with the sanction of the Lord Chancellor,” there is substituted“by the Lord Chancellor”., 
(3)In section 1(1) of the Public Trustee (Fees) Act [1957 (c.12)](/id/ukpga/1957/12?view=plain "Go to item of legislation") (orders fixingfees under section 9(1) of the 1906 Act)—, 
(a)for “Treasury” in each place there is substituted “Lord Chancellor”, and, 
(b)the words “, with the sanction of the Lord Chancellor,” are repealed., 
(4)In section 19(5)(b) of the Law of Property (Miscellaneous Provisions) Act[1994 (c. 36)](/id/ukpga/1994/36?view=plain "Go to item of legislation")(application of section 9(1), (3) and (4) of the 1906 Act), for “, (3) and(4)” there is substituted “and (3)”., 
## 3Short Title and extent, 
(1)This Act may be cited as the Public Trustee (Liability and Fees) Act 2002., 
(2)This Act does not extend to Scotland or Northern Ireland.,
