# Lords Spiritual (Women) Act 2015, 
# 2015 CHAPTER 18, 
An Act to make time-limited provision for vacancies among the Lords Spiritualto be filled by bishops who are women., 
[26th March 2015], 
Be it enacted by the Queen’s most Excellent Majesty, by and with the adviceand consent of the Lords Spiritual and Temporal, and Commons, in this presentParliament assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:—, 
## 1Vacancies among the Lords Spiritual, 
(1)This section applies where—, 
(a)a vacancy arises among the Lords Spiritual in the House of Lords in the 10years beginning with the day on which this Act comes into force,, 
(b)at the time the vacancy arises there is at least one eligible bishop who isa woman, and, 
(c)the person who would otherwise be entitled to fill the vacancy undersection 5 of the Bishoprics Act 1878 is a man., 
(2)If at the time the vacancy arises there is only one eligible bishop who isa woman, the vacancy is to be filled by the issue of writs of summons to her., 
(3)If at the time the vacancy arises there are two or more eligible bishopswho are women, the vacancy is to be filled by the issue of writs of summons tothe one whose election as a bishop of a diocese in England was confirmedfirst., 
(4)In this section “eligible bishop” means a bishop of a diocese in Englandwho is not yet entitled in that capacity to the issue of writs of summons., 
(5)The reference in subsection (1) to a vacancy does not include a vacancyarising by the avoidance of the see of Canterbury, York, London, Durham orWinchester., 
## 2Commencement, extent and short title, 
(1)This Act comes into force on the day Parliament first meets following thefirst parliamentary general election after this Act is passed., 
(2)This Act extends to England and Wales, Scotland and Northern Ireland., 
(3)This Act may be cited as the Lords Spiritual (Women) Act 2015.,
