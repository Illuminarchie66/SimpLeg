[![LegislationCrest](/images/crests/ukpga.gif)](/ukpga/2017/5/introduction/enacted?view=plain), # Commonwealth Development Corporation Act 2017, # 2017 CHAPTER 5, An Act to amend the amount of the limit in section 15 of the CommonwealthDevelopment Corporation Act 1999 on the government’s financial assistance., [23rd February 2017], Be it enacted by the Queen’s most Excellent Majesty, by and with the adviceand consent of the Lords Spiritual and Temporal, and Commons, in this presentParliament assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:—, ## 1Amount of the limit on government assistance, (1)Section 15 of the Commonwealth Development Corporation Act 1999 (limit ongovernment assistance) is amended as follows., (2)In subsection (1), for “£1,500 million” substitute “£6,000 million”., (3)After subsection (3) insert—, “(4)The Secretary of State may by regulations substitute for the amount forthe time being specified in subsection (1) a larger amount not exceeding£12,000 million., (5)Regulations made by the Secretary of State under this section are to bemade by statutory instrument., (6)A statutory instrument containing regulations under this section may not bemade unless a draft of the instrument has been laid before and approved by aresolution of the House of Commons.”, ## 2Short title, extent and commencement, (1)This Act may be cited as the Commonwealth Development Corporation Act 2017., (2)This Act extends to the whole of the United Kingdom., (3)This Act comes into force on the day on which it is passed.,
