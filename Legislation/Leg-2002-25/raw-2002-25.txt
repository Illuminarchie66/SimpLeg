section 107(4)(b) (criminal liability for making or dealing withinfringing articles, etc.), for “two” substitute “ten”., (3)In section 198(5)(b) (criminal liability for making, dealing with or effect in relation to any offence committedbefore the commencement of this section., ### 2Search warrants, (1)The 1988 Act is amended as follows., (2)In section 109 (search warrants: infringing
