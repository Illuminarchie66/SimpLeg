# Northern Ireland Arms Decommissioning (Amendment) Act 2002, 
# 2002 CHAPTER 6, 
An Act to provide for the extension of the amnesty period fixed by section 2of the Northern Ireland Arms Decommissioning Act 1997., 
[26th February 2002], 
Be it enacted by the Queen’s most Excellent Majesty, by and with the adviceand consent of the Lords Spiritual and Temporal, and Commons, in this presentParliament assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:—, 
## 1Extension of amnesty period, 
(1)Section 2 of the Northern Ireland Arms Decommissioning Act [1997 (c.7)](/id/ukpga/1997/7?view=plain "Go to item of legislation") (duration ofdecommissioning scheme) is amended as follows., 
(2)In subsection (2)(a), for “the first anniversary of the day on which thisAct is passed” substitute “27th February 2003”., 
(3)In subsection (3)(b), for “more than five years after the day on which thisAct is passed” substitute “later than 27th February 2007”., 
(4)If this section comes into force after 26th February 2002, a scheme whichwould at any time have been a decommissioning scheme for the purposes of theNorthern Ireland Arms Decommissioning Act 1997 had this section come intoforce on that date, is to be treated as having been such a scheme at thattime., 
## 2Short title, 
This Act may be cited as the Northern Ireland Arms Decommissioning (Amendment)Act 2002.,
