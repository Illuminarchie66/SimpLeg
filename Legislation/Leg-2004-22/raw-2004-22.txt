## 6Secretary of State to report on building stock, (1)The Secretary of State must—, (a)for the period of two years beginning with the commencement of thissection, and, (b)for each succeeding period and Wales., (2)A report under this section must (in particular) deal with—, (a)building regulations made during the period for any of those purposes;, (b)proposals current at the end of the period to
