# Non-Domestic Rating (Public Lavatories) Act 2021, 
# 2021 CHAPTER 13, 
An Act to confer relief from non-domestic rates for hereditaments in Englandand Wales that consist wholly or mainly of public lavatories; and forconnected purposes., 
[29th April 2021], 
Be it enacted by the Queen’s most Excellent Majesty, by and with the adviceand consent of the Lords Spiritual and Temporal, and Commons, in this presentParliament assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:—, 
## 1Relief from non-domestic rates for public lavatories, 
(1)In Part 3 of the Local Government Finance Act 1988 (non-domestic rating),section 43 (occupied hereditaments: liability) is amended as follows., 
(2)In subsection (4), after “(4E),” insert “(4I),”., 
(3)After subsection (4H) insert—, 
“(4I)Where, on a chargeable day, the hereditament consists wholly or mainly ofpublic lavatories, the chargeable amount for the chargeable day is zero.”, 
(4)After subsection (8C) insert—, 
“(8D)In relation to any hereditament in respect of which subsection (4I) andany of subsections (4A), (4E), (5) and (6A) each have effect on the dayconcerned, the chargeable amount shall be calculated in accordance withsubsection (4I).”, 
## 2Consequential amendments, 
(1)In Part 3 of the Local Government Finance Act 1988 (non-domestic rating),in section 67(7) (interpretation), after “43(4F)” insert “, (4I)”., 
(2)In section 13 of the Business Rate Supplements Act 2009 (liability tobusiness rate supplements: chargeable amount)—, 
(a)after subsection (3A) insert—, 
“(3B)If section 43(4I) of the 1988 Act (public lavatories) applies, the amountis zero.”;, 
(b)in subsection (9), after “(4F),” insert “(4I),”., 
(3)The Non-Domestic Rating (Chargeable Amounts) (England) Regulations 2016([S.I. 2016/1265](/id/uksi/2016/1265?view=plain "Go to item of legislation"))are amended in accordance with subsections (4) to (6)., 
(4)In regulation 6 (special authorities), in paragraph (12), after “under”insert “section 43(4I) of the Act (public lavatories: zero-rating) or”., 
(5)In regulation 11 (application of regulation 12: rules for determiningchargeable amount), in paragraph (3), after “under” insert “section 43(4I) ofthe Act (public lavatories: zero-rating) or”., 
(6)In Schedule 2 (splits and mergers), in paragraphs 4(1)(d) and 5(1)(d),after “under” insert “section 43(4I) of the Act (public lavatories: zero-rating) or”., 
(7)The amendments made by subsections (3) to (6) are without prejudice to anypower to make an order or regulations amending or revoking the regulationsmentioned in subsection (3)., 
## 3Financial provision, 
There is to be paid out of money provided by Parliament any increaseattributable to this Act in the sums payable under any other Act out of moneyso provided., 
## 4Extent, application and short title, 
(1)This Act extends to England and Wales., 
(2)The amendments made by this Act have effect in relation to financial yearsbeginning on or after 1 April 2020., 
(3)In subsection (2), “financial year” means a period of 12 months beginningwith 1 April., 
(4)This Act may be cited as the Non-Domestic Rating (Public Lavatories) Act2021.,
