[![LegislationCrest](/images/crests/ukpga.gif)](/ukpga/2017/25/introduction/enacted?view=plain), # Local Audit (Public Access to Documents) Act 2017, # 2017 CHAPTER 25, An Act to extend public access to certain local audit documents under section26 of the Local Audit and Accountability Act 2014., [27th April 2017], Be it enacted by the Queen’s most Excellent Majesty, by and with the adviceand consent of the Lords Spiritual and Temporal, and Commons, in this presentParliament assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:—, ## 1Inspection of accounting records by journalists and citizen journalists, (1)Section 26 of the Local Audit and Accountability Act 2014 (inspection ofdocuments etc) is amended as follows., (2)In subsection (1) (persons who can inspect accounting records and relateddocuments) after “persons interested” insert “or any journalist”., (3)After that subsection insert—, “(1A)In subsection (1) “journalist” means any person who produces forpublication journalistic material (whether paid to do so or otherwise).”, ## 2Commencement, extent and short title, (1)This Act comes into force at the end of the period of 2 months beginningwith the day on which this Act is passed., (2)This Act extends to England and Wales only., (3)This Act may be cited as the Local Audit (Public Access to Documents) Act2017.,
