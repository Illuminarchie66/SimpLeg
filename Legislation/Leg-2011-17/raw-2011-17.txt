# Coinage (Measurement) Act 2011, 
# 2011 CHAPTER 17, 
An Act to make provision about the arrangements for measuring the standardweight of coins., 
[3rd November 2011], 
Be it enacted by the Queen’s most Excellent Majesty, by and with the adviceand consent of the Lords Spiritual and Temporal, and Commons, in this presentParliament assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:—, 
## 1Amendment of Coinage Act 1971, 
(1)In section 1(6) of the Coinage Act 1971 (measurement of variation fromstandard weight)—, 
(a)omit “(other than a gold coin of a denomination mentioned in Schedule 1 tothis Act)”, and, 
(b)for “shall be measured as the average of a sample of not more than onekilogram of that coin” substitute “shall be measured in accordance withprovision made by proclamation under section 3”., 
(2)In section 3(1)(cd) of that Act (power to regulate coinage by proclamation)for “specified in Schedule 1 to this Act for a gold coin of a denominationmentioned in that Schedule” substitute “for coins”., 
## 2Extent, commencement and short title, 
(1)This Act extends to England and Wales, Scotland and Northern Ireland., 
(2)This Act comes into force at the end of the period of 2 months beginningwith the day on which it is passed., 
(3)This Act may be cited as the Coinage (Measurement) Act 2011.,
