[![LegislationCrest](/images/crests/ukpga.gif)](/ukpga/2019/10/introduction/enacted?view=plain), # Children Act 1989 (Amendment) (Female Genital Mutilation) Act 2019, # 2019 CHAPTER 10, An Act to amend the Children Act 1989 to provide that certain proceedingsunder Part 1 of Schedule 2 to the Female Genital Mutilation Act 2003 arefamily proceedings., [15th March 2019], Be it enacted by the Queen’s most Excellent Majesty, by and with the adviceand consent of the Lords Spiritual and Temporal, and Commons, in this presentParliament assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:—, ## 1Amendment to the Children Act 1989, (1)The Children Act 1989 is amended as follows., (2)In section 8 (child arrangements orders and other orders with respect tochildren), after subsection (4)(i) insert—, “(j)Part 1 of Schedule 2 to the Female Genital Mutilation Act 2003 (other thanparagraph 3 of that Schedule).”, ## 2Extent, commencement and short title, (1)This Act extends to England and Wales only., (2)This Act comes into force on the day on which it is passed., (3)This Act may be cited as the Children Act 1989 (Amendment) (Female GenitalMutilation) Act 2019.,
