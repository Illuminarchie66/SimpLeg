[![LegislationCrest](/images/crests/ukpga.gif)](/ukpga/2019/28/introduction/enacted?view=plain), # Census (Return Particulars and Removal of Penalties) Act 2019, # 2019 CHAPTER 28, An Act to amend the Census Act 1920 and the Census Act (Northern Ireland) 1969in relation to the provision of particulars about sexual orientation andgender identity., [8th October 2019], Be it enacted by the Queen’s most Excellent Majesty, by and with the adviceand consent of the Lords Spiritual and Temporal, and Commons, in this presentParliament assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:—, ## 1Amendment of the Census Act 1920, (1)The Census Act 1920 is amended as follows., (2)In the Schedule (matters in respect of which particulars may be required),after paragraph 5A insert—, “5BSexual orientation., 5CGender identity.”, (3)In section 8 (penalties), in subsection (1A), at the end insert “, sexualorientation or gender identity.”, ## 2Amendment of the Census Act (Northern Ireland) 1969, (1)The Census Act (Northern Ireland) 1969 is amended as follows., (2)In the Schedule (matters in respect of which particulars may be required),after paragraph 7 insert—, “7ASexual orientation., 7BGender identity.”, (3)In section 7 (penalties for offences), in subsection (3), after“profession” insert “or any particulars with respect to sexual orientation orgender identity”., ## 3Extent, commencement and short title, (1)Section 1 extends to England and Wales., (2)Section 2 extends to Northern Ireland., (3)This section extends to England and Wales and to Northern Ireland., (4)This Act comes into force on the day on which it is passed., (5)This Act may be cited as the Census (Return Particulars and Removal ofPenalties) Act 2019.,
